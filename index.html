About Us

Introduction

It's Possible Foundation is a registered non-profit organisation which is focused on youth leadership and entrepreneurship development. Our Focus involves a very strict selection process which is designed to develop talent and earmark young people for success by equipping Young people with a range of skills that will help them stay in school get education and Start their own enterprises. We seek to impart life skills for success and develop the entrepreneual spirit amongst in school learners and out of school youth from historically disadvantaged and urban communities by up-skilling and preparing them for the future. It's Possible Foundation was established aiming to touch millions of young people who will be assisted through designing, developing and executing youth development intervention that seek to address challenges that young people faces.We believe that the nurturing of young minds is about giving them hope through compelling personal stories shared by professional from various works of life or professions.
